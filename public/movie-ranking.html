Best Movies of All Time (In my opinion!)

  • Batman (2022)

    A fresh new take on the Batman and his world, perfect for old and new fans alike.

  • Spider-Man Across the Spider Verse

    A movie that features the struggles of an up and coming super hero trying to find his way. Paired with an incredible artsyle and attention to detail, this film is a treat for not only long time fans, but even people who are just being introduced to the character.

  • Life

    A film featuring comedy talents such as Eddie Murphy, Martin Lawrence, Bernie Mac, etc. An enjoyable ride all the way throughout.
